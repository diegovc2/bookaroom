Usage and tips
Find out more about using the Meeting Room System
Settings
These settings allow you to configure the base settings for your Meeting Room System.
Amenities Admin
Here you can configure reservable and permanent amenities that are available for all of your meeting rooms.
Branch Admin
This is a list of branches including map links, background images for the branch list and hours of operation.
Room Admin
Rooms are the physical spaces that exist in your building(s).
Containers Admin
Room Containers are the reservable spaces that are made up of at least one physical room or space. These can also include several rooms if they can be merged to form a larger room. Reserving a container reserves all of the rooms in that container, making them unavailable to reserve if they are already in use.
